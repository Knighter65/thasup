Ascolta 23 6451!

23 6451 is the groundbreaking debut album from Italian rapper and producer thasup (formerly known as Tha Supreme).
Released in 2019, this genre-defying project blends elements of trap, hip-hop, and experimental sounds, creating a distinct auditory experience that has captivated listeners worldwide.
With its cryptic title (a creative representation of "Le basi" in Leetspeak) and unconventional tracklist, 23 6451 showcases thasup's unique style and artistic vision.
The album features chart-topping hits like "blun7 a swishland" and collaborations with prominent artists such as Marracash, Salmo, and Fabri Fibra.
Through introspective lyrics, innovative beats, and his signature vocal delivery, thasup redefines the boundaries of modern rap, making 23 6451 an essential listen for fans of cutting-edge music.
Dive into 23 6451 and discover why thasup is one of Italy's most influential contemporary artists.
