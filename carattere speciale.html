Carattere Speciale is the highly anticipated sophomore album from Italian rapper and producer thasup.
Released in 2022, the album further cements thasup's position as a visionary in the music industry.
With its distinctive production style, cryptic lyrics, and genre-blending approach, Carattere Speciale pushes the boundaries of modern music.
The project includes standout tracks like "s!r!" and "m%n," along with collaborations with major artists such as Tiziano Ferro and Lazza.
Through this album, thasup explores themes of identity, emotion, and the complexity of human connection, all wrapped in his signature sound.
Carattere Speciale is a must-listen for fans of innovative, boundary-breaking music.
